FAQ


Sign up

I tried to sign up but I can not click the button “Sign up”. What is wrong?

Make sure you fill out the whole form and then try again. Especially make sure that the email address is correct, the house number has numbers and maximum on letter, the ZIP is a 4-digit code and the passwords are equal as well as at least 6 characters long.


I tried to sign up but I get the message “Failed to register”. Why does it not work?

The email you use is already registered. So try to login or reset your password on the login page. Or make sure you wrote your email the correct way and try again. Otherwise you are asked to use a different email address.


I signed up, but never got an email.

Please make sure to refresh your email as well as your spam. It sometimes takes up to 10 minutes for you to receive our email. If you haven’t received it till then, you can use the “send Mail again” button which is located on the Login page. Just type in your email and your password and hit the login button. A message will be displayed saying please check your emails and next to it there is a button saying “Send Mail again”. Click that button and enter your email address.


Login

I forgot my password what should I do?

Just click on forgot password in order to get a email with a link to reset your password. It’s that easy.


I get the failure “Please verify your email address”. Why is the login not working?

After you have signed yourself up, you need to verify by opening the link you get from us in the email in a new tab. You will then be told you can now login and proceed to login.


Creating an event service

I can not create an event service because I can not click on the button.

Make sure you have filled out all the mandatory fields (all the fields except picture and special requirements). Also make sure your picture is not to big and in jpg or pgn format. Then try again.


I created an event service but it is not shown in the profile. What went wrong?

Was there a pop up asking you, if you want to go to the search page or to your profile page. If so your event service should have been created. We recommend to refresh the page if you can’t see your service. Otherwise we are sorry. Please try again. If it really does not work after a few tries, we would be very grateful if you could let us know about your problems in creating an event service. Thank you.


General

How can I add an event service to the favorite services?

Just click on the star in the detail page.


Where can I find my favorite event services?

You can find them in your profile page.


Is there a way to see all my event services ?

Yes there is just go to your profile. They are all listed there. If you want to see them in detail just click on the card.


Can I update my event service and can I delete one of them?

Just click on the profile icon to get to the profile page, scroll through the list and click on the service you want to update. In the detail page you see a button saying update. That’s where you can update your service.


How many event services can I offer?

You are able to offer up to 5 event services.


How much does it cost to use Eventdoo?

It is absolutely free. Have fun planning your event or offering your event services.


Is there a way to report inappropriate services?

Yes there is. Just open the detail page of the event service and click on the button saying report.
